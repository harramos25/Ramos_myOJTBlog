April 13–17, 2026

Preparation and System Turnover Week

This week was dedicated to preparing, finalizing, and presenting our work. We organized the presentation, reviewed the system carefully, practiced for the turnover, and successfully presented and turned over the NCIP IX Job Portal to the agency. It was the most important and fulfilling week of my OJT.

April 13: Preparing Turnover Materials

We started preparing our turnover materials today. I focused on organizing the PowerPoint presentation and reviewing the system details that we needed to explain clearly. This day felt important because it marked the beginning of wrapping up all our hard work into something presentable.

April 14: Refining Presentation Details

I continued refining the PowerPoint slides while carefully reviewing the system. I made sure that every feature, flow, and function was easy to understand when presented. It required attention to detail to ensure that nothing important was missed.

April 15: Final Edits and Practice

This day was for finalizing the presentation. I did last-minute edits, checked the slides repeatedly, and practiced how we would explain the system during the turnover. Preparation gave me confidence that we were ready.

April 16: Major Milestone — System Turnover

We successfully presented our PowerPoint and officially turned over the NCIP IX Job Portal system to the agency. It felt rewarding to finally present the system we worked on for months. This day marked a big milestone in my OJT journey.

April 17: Post-Turnover Organization

This was another work-from-home day. I used the time to stay updated and make sure all remaining files and documents were properly organized after the turnover.
