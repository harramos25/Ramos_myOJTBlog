Bagong Pilipinas

"Bagong Pilipinas" (New Philippines) is a national vision emphasizing inclusive growth, social justice, and sustainable development for all Filipinos. NCIP aligns with this vision by ensuring that Indigenous Peoples—who are often marginalized—are included in nation-building, development programs, and governance. Through NCIP, IP communities are empowered to contribute to the country's progress, making them active participants in the ideals of a "Bagong Pilipinas."

About NCIP

The National Commission on Indigenous Peoples (NCIP) is the Philippine government agency responsible for protecting and promoting the rights and welfare of Indigenous Peoples. NCIP XI focuses on the development of IP communities in Region XI through programs, policies, and initiatives that respect culture, heritage, and traditions.
