December 9–10, 2025

First Day at NCIP XI + Beginning the System Development

December 9, 2025 — First Day at NCIP XI

We wore our Type A uniform and reported for our first day. It was calm — mostly orientation and observation at first. Afterward, all six OJT students were grouped and assigned projects. Our team, Diskar-Tech, was instructed to build a Web-Based Job Application System for NCIP XI.

HR Ma'am Nor clearly explained what she needed, which helped us visualize the system flow. After the orientation, my team started planning the structure and features of the system.

December 10, 2025 — Layouting Begins

We started working on the system layout and interface design — wireframing, choosing colors that match NCIP's identity, and planning the user journey.

Once the layout was ready, we went to Ma'am Nor to present our design. She was very patient and kind while giving feedback. We revised what needed to be adjusted based on her instructions.
