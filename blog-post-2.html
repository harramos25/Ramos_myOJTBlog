December 3–5, 2025

Paperwork, Interviews, and A Workshop on Professionalism

December 3, 2025 – Morning

Paperwork day. Agreements, consent forms, endorsements — the entire process felt like navigating a maze. At first, it was overwhelming, but I realized how essential this step is. These documents protect both the student and the company, ensuring expectations and responsibilities are clear. Securing everything early helped me feel more prepared and confident.

We went to our dean to secure our endorsement letter, and the experience felt like the official start of our internship journey.

December 3, 2025 – Afternoon

We headed to the NCIP HR Office to apply for our OJT placement. We were interviewed by the HR team — I was nervous at first but the interview went smoothly. After the interview, we even took a photo outside the NCIP building.

December 5, 2025 — Workplace Etiquette Workshop

We attended the "CCS Practicum: Workshop on Office Workplace Etiquette" at SCC AVR, with Ms. Melanie Gay B. Barnido, RGC as the guest speaker. She taught us about professionalism, workplace manners, and how we should behave in our designated agencies.

Before the lecture, she held an interactive activity where we formed two circles facing each other. When the music stopped, we asked our partners questions like:

  • "What's your favorite tool/app and why?"
  • "Why did you choose IT?"

It was fun and energizing — a great way to break the tension.

Later, we were grouped to do role-playing. My group performed a scenario about interns who didn't research about their assigned agency. After the skit, we discussed the correct behavior expected in real workplace situations.

At the end of the workshop, we each received a certificate of participation.
