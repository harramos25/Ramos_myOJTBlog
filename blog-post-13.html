March 3 – March 6, 2026

Balancing Presence and Responsibility

Despite light workloads, I stayed consistent in reporting to the office, assisted with document preparation, and handled physical and errand tasks responsibly.

March 3, 2026 — Consistency in Presence

Even though I was absent in the morning because of work, I still chose to report to the office in the afternoon. I didn’t want to waste the remaining time of the day. There were no major tasks assigned, but I stayed present and available. Showing up, even for half a day, reminded me that consistency still matters.

March 4, 2026 — Productive Free Time

I was again absent in the morning due to personal errands, but I reported in the afternoon. Since there were no urgent tasks, I used my time to fix bugs in the OJT DTR system. Instead of staying idle, I focused on improving the system and making sure it runs smoother. Quiet days became my coding days.

March 5, 2026 — Administrative Assistance

This day was mostly about assisting with documents. We punched holes in stacks of papers so they could be properly filed in folders. It was a simple task, but it reminded me how much manual work still happens inside an office setting.

March 6, 2026 — Physical Tasks and Responsibility

We rearranged tables and adjusted the office setup, which required physical effort. I was also sent on an errand to buy a cold and hot faucet for the water dispenser. After buying it, I was instructed to install it myself. It was not related to IT work, but I handled it responsibly and finished the task.
