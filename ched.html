The Commission on Higher Education (CHED)

About CHED

The Commission on Higher Education (CHED) plays a vital role in ensuring that Saint Columban College (SCC) maintains high academic standards and provides quality education to its students. As a Catholic educational institution, SCC adheres to CHED's regulations, guaranteeing that its programs, faculty, and facilities meet national higher education standards.

CHED oversees the curriculum, course offerings, and tuition fees, ensuring SCC's programs remain competitive and aligned with national and global academic trends. By following CHED guidelines, SCC upholds academic excellence, institutional integrity, and student welfare.
