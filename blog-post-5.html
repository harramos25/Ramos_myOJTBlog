January 5–9, 2026

Back in Motion: Progress After the Holiday Break

January 6, 2026 — System Testing with HR

Today, we presented the NCIP IX Job Portal to the HR office for testing. We walked them through the design and system flow, and I was relieved when Ma’am Nor expressed her satisfaction with the results. She also requested a few additional features, which we carefully noted. This experience boosted my confidence and reminded me how valuable user feedback is in system development.

January 7, 2026 — MOA Signing and a Nostalgic Visit

We were excused from duty today to visit Saint Columban College Buenavista for the MOA signing. It was heartwarming to see our dean, Sir Hardy, and Sir Mark again. Our conversation brought a sense of nostalgia, reminding me of how far we’ve come since our early college days. After the signing, we returned to the office, carrying both renewed motivation and gratitude.

January 8, 2026 — Administrative Task and MOA Signing

I was assigned to encode a Secretary’s Certificate, which helped me gain more exposure to administrative tasks. After completing it, we proceeded to Ma’am Nor for the MOA signing. It was another reminder that OJT isn’t only about technical skills, but also about responsibility and professionalism.

January 9, 2026 — Quiet but Productive Day

With fewer tasks assigned today, I focused on continuing improvements for the NCIP Job Portal project. Even on quieter days, I learned that initiative and self-direction are important traits in the workplace.
