Panagdait! I'm Harra

IT Student | Front-end Developer | Quality Assurance

A 22-year-old Information Technology student from Saint Columban College. I was raised in Poblacion, Lapuyan, Zamboanga Peninsula, and I am currently staying in Barangay Santo Niño, Pagadian City while completing my studies and On-the-Job Training at the National Commission on Indigenous Peoples (NCIP) Region IX.

My journey in IT has been shaped by a strong passion for UI/UX design, system layouting, and improving user experience. I love the challenge of transforming ideas and concepts into clean, intuitive, and functional interfaces. Designing wireframes, crafting user flows, and enhancing usability are the parts of IT where I feel most in my element.

Beyond design, I'm continuously expanding my skills in front-end development, QA testing, documentation, and user-focused system planning. Working on academic projects—such as D'Marsians, FoodTrack, and PawPointment—has taught me how to collaborate effectively, understand user needs, and bridge creativity with technical structure.

My OJT experience at NCIP has further shaped me professionally. Being in a government environment has taught me the importance of accuracy, patience, organization, and communication. Every day, I learn more about how technology supports real processes, and how even small tasks contribute to a larger mission of public service.

This page is a closer look into my journey — my skills, achievements, and the experiences that helped me grow both personally and professionally. I may still be a student, but I'm driven, adaptable, and committed to becoming an IT professional who combines technical skill, creativity, and a genuine passion for meaningful work.

— Harra
