November 24–28, 2025

A Slow Start, but Finally Enrolled

The second semester officially opened on November 24, but unlike most students who went straight back to class, our team was still buried in capstone requirements. We were busy polishing our documents, reviewing corrections from our technical panelists, and making sure everything was aligned before submission.

It was a stressful but meaningful week — the kind that reminds you how close you are to finishing your degree, and how important teamwork is during tight deadlines.

Finally, on November 28, 2025, my team Diskar-Tech and I officially enrolled for the second semester. We also took a picture with our Certificate of Enrollment, marking both relief and excitement as we prepared for the next big chapter: our OJT journey.
