Research Festival 2025

A page from my research journey

November 7, 2025

Today was one of those days I know I'll remember for a long time. The Research Festival 2025 at Saint Columban College wasn't just an academic event—it felt like a celebration of ideas, teamwork, and the courage to share what we've worked so hard for.

With the theme "Innovation Minds, Impactful Research: Empowering Change Through Discovery," the atmosphere was filled with creativity and passion. Everywhere I looked, students were proudly presenting their work, exchanging ideas, and supporting one another. It reminded me why research is more than just papers and deadlines—it's about discovery, growth, and making a difference.

Together with my team, Diskar-Tech, I had the opportunity to present our research output titled "D'Marsians: Web-Based Admission & Progress Tracking with Real-Time Visualization System." Standing there, explaining our system, I felt a mix of nervousness and excitement. All the late nights, revisions, and teamwork finally made sense in that moment.

What made this day even more meaningful was knowing that our dedication and collaboration contributed to the success of the festival. Being part of an event that inspires innovation and encourages young minds to think beyond limits was truly fulfilling.

The Research Festival 2025, held at the Youth Development Center (YDC) of SCC in Pagadian City, was more than a requirement—it was an experience. One that strengthened my confidence, deepened my love for learning, and reminded me that meaningful ideas have the power to create impact when shared with others.

Research Presented

Title: D'Marsians: Web-Based Admission & Progress Tracking with Real-Time Visualization System
Event: Research Festival 2025
Date: November 7, 2025
Venue: SCC – Youth Development Center
