Quiet Closing Days and Completion of OJT Hours

With many staff attending a seminar and some interns already finished, the office became quiet with minimal tasks. These were my final on-site days before completing the remaining hours through work from home, marking the calm closing of my OJT journey.

April 20–21: A Quiet Office Environment

Starting Monday, the office felt much quieter. Many staff members were away attending the CES seminar, and several co-interns had already completed their hours. Tasks were minimal, but I remained professional and available for any office support needed.

April 22: Continued Learning and Handbook Review

Another slow day in terms of technical tasks. I maximized the quiet time by reviewing the SCC Student Handbook, ensuring I stayed productive by sharpening my knowledge on institutional guidelines as I prepared for graduation and post-internship requirements.

April 23: Final On-site Duty

With very few people in the office, this was my final day of physical presence at NCIP. It was a calm and reflective closing to my on-site internship experience. My remaining hours would be fulfilled remotely, wrapping up the documentation phase of my journey.

April 24: OJT Completion and Reflection

Working from home today marked the official completion of my required OJT hours. I spent the day finalizing my documentation, organizing files, and reflecting on the incredible growth—both technical and professional—that I've experienced over the past few months.
